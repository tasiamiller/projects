The God of Highschool

2020 PG-13 1 Season

God of High School follows a 17-year-old kid named Jin Mori as he competes in a tournament called "The God of High School." Contestants all over the world battle to determine who is the strongest high schooler and for a chance to get their deepest wish granted.

Creator: Park Yongje, Yoshimura Kiyoko

Cast: Tatsumaru Tachibana, Kentaro Kumagai, Ayaka Ohashi, Sean Chiplock, Robbie Daymond, Veronica Taylor

Genre: Action, Webtoon Adaptation,Comedy, Martial Arts, Fantasy,Shounen
